FLOOD THE ZONE — Top-30 Matchup Guide

Team: Pelipper / Mega Swampert / Sneasler / Meowscarada / Rotom-Wash / Sinistcha. Every effectiveness below is computed from the type chart, not hand-typed. Core principle: keep rain up — it doubles your Water and cancels enemy sun.

Read this first — three things raw type-eff doesn't show

  1. Water is HALVED in enemy sun. So the "Water 2×" answers vs Charizard-Y / Torkoal / Talonflame are only neutral while their sun is up. Use Thunderbolt / Ice Punch (weather-proof) until your Pelipper flips rain, then Water nukes (1.5× rain × 2× type = 3×).
  2. Immunities eat some "answers": Gholdengo is immune to Sneasler's Fighting AND Poison (Ghost/Steel). Sableye is immune to Close Combat (Ghost). The table already excludes these.
  3. Priority/ability blocks: Tsareena Queenly Majesty blocks your Sucker Punch/Fake Out; Rotom/Gholdengo etc. — check before relying on priority.

Gameplan

  • Lead Pelipper + a breaker (Sneasler/Meowscarada). Rain turn 1, Tailwind turn 2 if you need the speed race.
  • Rain is the team. Vs sun/snow/sand, re-set with Pelipper — whoever's weather activates last wins. Bank a Pelipper switch.
  • Swift Swim Swampert doubles speed in rain — fastest breaker once it's up.
  • Sinistcha Rage Powder buys a turn; Strength Sap neuters a physical attacker; Rotom Will-O-Wisp enables Hex and cripples physicals.
  • Priority = Meowscarada Sucker Punch, but it fails into Queenly Majesty and any non-attacking turn.
