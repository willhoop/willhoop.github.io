Structural threats (not a single mon)

  • Trick Room (Farigiraf, Sinistcha, Torkoal, Mawile, Mudsdale/Stamina cores): 4 of your 6 are fast. Kill the setter pre-TR (it moves last, -7 priority) or stall 5 turns with Protect/Wide Guard/pivots.
  • Weather wars (Zard-Y, Torkoal, Ninetales-A, Tyranitar): you have 2 rain sources (Drizzle re-entry + priority Rain Dance) — you win these if Pelipper and Sableye live.
  • Stamina stall (Mudsdale, Archaludon): Body Press scales off their boosted Def. Don't feed contact hits; use special (Hurricane, Rising Voltage) or one big Helping Hand nuke. You saw this one first-hand.
  • Dark-types (Kingambit, Grimmsnarl, Hydreigon, Sableye, Incineroar): all immune to Sableye's Prankster status (Encore!). Helping Hand and Rain Dance still work (ally/field targets).
  • Armor Tail / Good as Gold / Clear Body: Farigiraf blocks your priority, Gholdengo blocks Parting Shot, Metagross ignores Intimidate. Know which support buttons are dead before you click them.
