Standing lessons

  1. Bring-4 by matchup, not autopilot. Ghold is mandatory vs Staraptor teams. Blaziken vs TTar/sand.
  2. Never HJK into a probable Protect — Taunt first or target elsewhere.
  3. Mega Staraptor inverts stat drops — Intimidate/Charm feed it post-mega.
  4. Foul Play punishes Tsareena (189 Atk) and hits Ghold 2x.
  5. Contact into Baneful Bunker = poison.
  6. Sucker Punch discipline: low-HP target behind likely Protect → click Kowtow.
  7. Enemy Gambits keep staying home vs the lure — expect 4v5 on their side.
  8. Good as Gold blocks YOUR Helping Hand too — never target Gholdengo with status, even friendly.
  9. Blaziken never switches into rain — Weather Ball/Water is 2x and rain-boosted.
  10. Vs rain: Whimsicott (Tailwind/Encore) is a required bring for the speed war.
